CLUBLOG

Develop and Design

Imagine hundreds of people working on the same events calendar. Hundreds of people trying to add and edit their own events.
How do you ensure that a user can add and edit their own events without changing or deleting others?
This is Club Log. It allows hundreds and even thousnds of people to schedule their own events in one calendar, while maintaining the details of existing entries.

With the lastest technologies, Club Log is fast and secure.
